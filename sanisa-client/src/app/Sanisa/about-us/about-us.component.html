Empowering The Rural Women

Sa-Ni-Sa provides employment opportunities to under-privileged and needy women to enable them become independent. Training is provided to manufacture various products and corporate gift packs under its brands ‘Chaakri’, ‘Divinite’, ‘Puro’, ‘Udaan’ and ‘All Things Yum’.

Creating Employment Opportunities For Women’s Lives

At Sa-Ni-Sa, we believe in the power of opportunity. By providing meaningful employment and training to underprivileged women, we not only help them gain financial independence but also empower them to build better futures for themselves and their families. Our initiatives are designed to uplift women from challenging circumstances, enabling them to achieve their full potential and contribute positively to their communities. Together, we are making a lasting impact, one job at a time.
